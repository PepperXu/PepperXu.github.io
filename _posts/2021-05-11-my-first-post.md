I intend to make this a place for publicizing my professional or academic status, or sharing the idea that may inspire other people. I believe that I should generally avoid self-indulgence or delusion in my writings, which is some constraints mostly overlooked nowadays. But that does not mean that I would compromise my subjective point of view. In fact, I enjoy being a part of the community of human-being that share so many common traits while holding some originalities to distinguish myself. I am willing to acknowledge the limitations of myself when I am made aware of them.

The world isn’t a perfect place and whether people can make it better is often not a valid question. There are painful contradictions in the stories we tell today to describe the happenings. In terms of telling the story, it needs great courage to make it bare, simple and short. It is like line drawings that show the spirit rather than the likeness. It is paramount for me to pursuit the essence of love and desire, which is not just a personal issue but a political one. Beyond all these, it is necessary to note the way my writing epitomize my subjectivity is by three pillars: the feelings, the thoughts and the expressions.

See you in the future.
